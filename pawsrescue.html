MISSION

Mission: Our nonprofit is passionately committed to transforming the lives of street dogs in Hyderabad. We're driven by a simple yet powerful goal: to provide comprehensive support and care to these vulnerable animals. Through initiatives like regular food distribution, medical assistance, adoption facilitation, and sterilization campaigns, we're working hard to address the challenges faced by street dogs and improve their well-being. By fostering a community of compassion and responsibility, we believe we can create lasting change for Hyderabad's street dogs. Join us in our mission to build a brighter future for them, one step at a time.

EVENTS

Dog walk-athon


Charity dog walk event with fun treats and a chance for our furry friends to make playmates. Participants can walk or run with their dogs. We encourage participants to collect pledges or donations for the NGO.

Dog show


The annual dog show, with different competitions, serves as a key fundraiser, supporting our street dog initiative by funding essential medical treatments, spaying/neutering, and adoption services. The event raises awareness about street dogs, encouraging community involvement and adoption.

PROGRAMS

Spay/Neuter Clinics:


Our organization is proud to offer regular spay/neuter clinics aimed at controlling the street dog population in Hyderabad. Through these clinics, we provide free or low-cost spaying and neutering services to ensure a healthier and more manageable street dog population. By preventing unplanned litters, we're making a significant impact on the well-being of street dogs and the community at large.

Mobile Veterinary Care:

With our mobile veterinary clinic initiative, we're bringing essential medical care directly to the streets of Hyderabad. Our team of dedicated veterinarians travels to different areas, offering vaccinations, deworming, and treatment for injuries and illnesses to street dogs who may not have access to veterinary services otherwise. Through this program, we're not only improving the health of individual dogs but also building trust and relationships within the community.

Street Dog Feeding Program:


Our organization runs a dedicated street dog feeding program aimed at ensuring that no street dog in Hyderabad goes hungry. Through this initiative, we distribute nutritious food to street dogs across various locations in the city on a regular basis. By providing consistent access to food, we not only alleviate hunger but also improve the overall health and well-being of these animals. Additionally, our feeding program serves as a point of contact for identifying and addressing other needs, such as medical care and adoption opportunities. Together, we're making a tangible difference in the lives of Hyderabad's street dogs, one meal at a time.
